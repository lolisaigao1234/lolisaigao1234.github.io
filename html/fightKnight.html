The knight celebrated with his whole soul and body. He jumped up and down, cheering "hooray" with some huge laughs. After several minutes, you noticed that he gradually stopped what he was doing. The knight suddenly started speaking.

"Yes, after so many years, after such a long wait, after such a help from you, I had my revenge on this person. " The knight said all of these with full strength, you can even hear the quiver in his voice.

Now I should reveal my true identity. I am the former Duke that was assassinated by this obnoxious brave man. I am the justice. I raised the tax on my own behave, and the villagers should be thankful for me to give them so long time for a pleasant life.

"I have shared my secret, so you could not live."

The knight swung his sword towards you. But you deflected his sword without a hesitation and stabbed your little knife into the knight's body.

"No, My plans, My World, My Reign, All I Want. No, This Could Not Be True. No. You Must Be Joking. I Refuse To Acknowledge This."

The knight, like the Duke, is also dead. Nothing was left.
