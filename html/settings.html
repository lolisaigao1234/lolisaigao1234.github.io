Once upon a time, there was a duke that is really nice to the people at his land. He had given food to the poor people in the land. He had shared his house with travellers who was eagered to find a place to live. He had done many respectful stuff that many others could not image.

However, one day, without much indication, he acted like another person that everyone could not believe. He raised the tax at his territory. He built up an huge house for himself and his maids to live, which I believe you have an idea what it is. Everyone is desparate and curious that where is their beloved duke.

Months passed, the people at his land could not bear the torture and the high tax that the duke planted on them. The major at the little village decided to recruit someone to take down the duke and set the villagers free.

The hero, the brave man, the spectacular whose name is Bob came and decided to take the quest. He sneaked in the house where the duke lives. He silently assassinated the duke for justice.

Everyone thought that their miserable life will end from now on, which is what happened in the early stage.

Nonetheless, the happy life for the villagers only lasted for a few weeks. The hero Bob was elected to become the new duke to reign this area, and he, same as the last Duke, decided to raise taxes on the villagers.

The villagers regreted that they started the quest, as they actually did not get what they want. They just got another tyrant.

The good news is that Bob, a few days ago, left the house and went into a dungeon for some secret stuff. He even left everything back in the house. Thus, my request to you, my traveller, is to kill Bob and give the villagers true peace and happiness.
